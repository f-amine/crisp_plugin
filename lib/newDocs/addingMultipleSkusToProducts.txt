
## Adding Multiple SKUs to One Product Page

### Issue
How to add two SKUs to one product page in Lightfunnels.

### Solution
1. **Use Variants**:
   - To add multiple SKUs to a single product page, utilize the product variant feature.
   - Each variant can have its own unique SKU.

2. **Implementation**:
   - Create a product and add variants under the product settings.
   - Assign different SKUs to each variant as needed.

### Testing
- After setting up the variants and SKUs, test the product page to ensure that the SKUs are correctly displayed and functioning.
