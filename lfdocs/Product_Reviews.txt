Title: Product Reviews

URL Source: https://docs.lightfunnels.com/product-reviews/

Published 

Markdown Content:
Reviews are one of the best ways to raise social proof for your products and increase your conversion rates.

You can access your reviews by going to `Products` on the sidebar then choosing `Reviews`. Here you can see all the reviews you have on your account. You can use the dropdown at the top to filter reviews by product.

Adding a Review[](#adding-a-review)
-----------------------------------

To add a review, click `Add Review`, select a product and fill the form with the review data.

Editing or Deleting a Review[](#editing-or-deleting-a-review)
-------------------------------------------------------------

To edit a review, click on the review content. A review modal will appear. Here you can edit the form and save or completely delete the review.
