Title: Clarity Integration

URL Source: https://docs.lightfunnels.com/clarity-integration/

Published 

Markdown Content:
Clarity is a free, easy-to-use tool that captures how real people actually use your site. Setting up Clarity on Lightfunnels is easy and you'll start getting data in minutes.

Follow these steps to get Clarity setup on your Lightfunnels account.

1.  Go to your Clarity account -> Settings -> Overview and copy the Project ID
2.  Log in to your Lightfunnels account and go to the Apps tab
3.  Look for the Clarity app and click Install then open the app
4.  Paste the project ID and click install
5.  You're all set!
