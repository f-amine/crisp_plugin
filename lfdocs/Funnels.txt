Home Funnel[](#home-funnel)
---------------------------

The home funnel is the funnel the visitors will see after visiting the root of your domain name. For example, `example.com` or `accountname.mylightfunnels.com`.

Default Theme[](#default-theme)
-------------------------------

The default funnel is the theme that is automatically used when you view your product in the storefront.
