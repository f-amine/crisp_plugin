Integrations in LightFunnels allow you to send information to other platforms when something occurs in your account.

### Currently supported integration flows

*   Send lead information when a new lead is collected. `Lead / Created`.
*   Send order and customer information when an order is created. `Order / Created`.
*   Send form information when a contact form is submitted. `Form / Submitted`.
