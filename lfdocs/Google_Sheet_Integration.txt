Title: Google Sheet Integration

URL Source: https://docs.lightfunnels.com/google-sheet/

Published 

Markdown Content:
Google sheet one of the perfect tools you can use for dropshipping and ecommerce. This integration allows you to sync your leads and sales automatically from your funnels to your sheets in real time. This way, you can keep track of your progress and make data-driven decisions to improve your business.

### How does it work?[](#how-does-it-work)

Log in to your account and go to Apps
