Title: Discounts

URL Source: https://docs.lightfunnels.com/discounts/

Published 

Markdown Content:
You can offer discount codes to your customers and prospects.

To create a discount, click on `Discounts` then `New Discount`.

`Code` - You can generate a random code automatically or enter a custom code.

`Type` - Whether the discount will reduce the price by a percentage or by a fixed amount

`Set start and end dates` - The date range during which this discount will be valid.

`Limit discount` - Limit the number of times the discount can be used.

`One time usage per user` - Only allow the discount to be used once by customer identified by their email and / or phone number.

Click `Save`.

You can now share the code with your customers via emails or ads.
